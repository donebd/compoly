<p><b>Grzegorz Guzik</b> (born 20 August 1991 in <a href="/wiki/Sucha_Beskidzka" title="Sucha Beskidzka">Sucha Beskidzka</a>) is a Polish <a href="/wiki/Biathlon" title="Biathlon">biathlete</a>. He competed at the <a href="/wiki/Biathlon_World_Championships_2013" title="Biathlon World Championships 2013">Biathlon World Championships 2013</a> in <a href="/wiki/Nove_Mesto_na_Morave" class="mw-redirect" title="Nove Mesto na Morave">Nove Mesto na Morave</a>. He competed at the <a href="/wiki/2014_Winter_Olympics" title="2014 Winter Olympics">2014 Winter Olympics</a> in <a href="/wiki/Sochi" title="Sochi">Sochi</a>, in <a href="/wiki/Biathlon_at_the_2014_Winter_Olympics_%E2%80%93_Men%27s_sprint" title="Biathlon at the 2014 Winter Olympics – Men&#39;s sprint">sprint</a> and <a href="/wiki/Biathlon_at_the_2014_Winter_Olympics_%E2%80%93_Men%27s_individual" title="Biathlon at the 2014 Winter Olympics – Men&#39;s individual">individual</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-sochi_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-sochi-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In July 2014, Guzik married fellow biathlete <a href="/wiki/Krystyna_Guzik" title="Krystyna Guzik">Krystyna Pałka</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
