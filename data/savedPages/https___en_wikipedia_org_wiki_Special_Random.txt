<p><b>Nizhneyablochny</b> (<a href="/wiki/Russian_language" title="Russian language">Russian</a>: <span lang="ru">Нижнеяблочный</span>) is a <a href="/wiki/Types_of_inhabited_localities_in_Russia" class="mw-redirect" title="Types of inhabited localities in Russia">rural locality</a> (a <a href="/wiki/Village#Russia" title="Village">khutor</a>) in <a href="/wiki/Kotelnikovsky_District" title="Kotelnikovsky District">Kotelnikovsky District</a>, <a href="/wiki/Volgograd_Oblast" title="Volgograd Oblast">Volgograd Oblast</a>, Russia. The population was 1,174 as of 2010.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> There are 12 streets.
</p>
